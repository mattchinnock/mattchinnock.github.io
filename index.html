Critical Thinking

My name is Matt Chinnock, a British born techie from Honolulu, Hawaii. I've helped shape the future of the ice industry, redfinded lead generation in Higher Education, and co-founded an integration platform that connects Salesforce.com natively to other systems. I've worked in factories, offices, accelerators and incubators across the country including the tech capital of the world, San Francisco.

My primary objective is to work on projects that inspire, innovate and improve the lives of others. This is achieved using the following three funamentals:

User driven design

Development starts not in an IDE but with user personas, crafting stories, use cases and simplifying business requirements into digestible tasks. Meaningful structure behind why we build things keeps projects on time and true to their business requirements.

Creative problem solving

Finding solutions where there were none through collobaration and innovation. There may be many ways to solve a problem; I find that the best ones happen at 6pm on Friday night around whiteboard and a few beers!

Agile Excecution

I practice Agile, using tools such as Jira and Bitbucket to seemlessly move between issue tracking, prioritization, development and testing cycles.
